HomeTown

Talking about my hometown, I am from the great country of India, the southern state of Karnataka and the capital city of Bangalore. I have lived there my entire life. During my childhood days, it used to be called the "Green City". But as years passed by, lot of people migrated to the south from all across the country. And the IT boom happened. Suddenly the city had a lot of population, lot of buildings and the "Green" seemed to be vanishing.

Having to stay far away from my hometown is the hardest part! I still love my city, lot of memories attached, always wanting to go back!
